    User Guide

    Getting Started

    Welcome to the Barangay Management System! This guide will help you understand how to use all features of the system effectively.

    The Barangay Management System provides tools to manage:

    • Resident information
    • Barangay officials
    • Document issuance and tracking
    • Barangay projects

    Managing Residents

    The Residents module helps you keep track of all people living in your barangay.

    Adding a New Resident

    1. Go to the Residents page
    2. Click on "Add New Resident"
    3. Fill in all required information
    4. Click "Save Resident" to complete the process

    Viewing and Editing Resident Information

    To view a resident's complete profile:

    1. Navigate to the Residents page
    2. Click the "View" icon next to the resident's name
    3. From the resident's profile, you can also edit their information or issue documents for them

    Document Management

    The Documents module allows you to issue, track, and manage barangay documents.

    Issuing a Document

    1. Go to the Documents page and click "Issue New Document"
    2. Select the resident from the dropdown list
    3. Choose the document type (Barangay Clearance, Certificate of Residency, etc.)
    4. Enter the purpose for the document
    5. Click "Issue Document" to complete the process

    Printing Documents

    To print a document:

    1. Find the document in the Documents list
    2. Click the "Print" icon
    3. A printer-friendly version will open in a new window
    4. Use your browser's print function to print the document

    Managing Officials

    The Officials module helps you keep track of all elected and appointed barangay officials.

    Recording Official Information

    1. Go to the Officials page
    2. Click "Add New Official"
    3. Fill in the official's details including their position and term dates
    4. Click "Save Official" to complete the process

    The system automatically marks officials as "Current" or "Past" based on their term dates.

    Project Management

    The Projects module helps you track barangay projects and their status.

    Adding a New Project

    1. Go to the Projects page
    2. Click "Add New Project"
    3. Enter the project name, description, dates, budget, and current status
    4. Click "Save Project" to record the project

    Updating Project Status

    As projects progress, remember to update their status:

    1. Find the project in the Projects list
    2. Click the "Edit" icon
    3. Update the status and any other relevant information
    4. Save your changes

    Need More Help?

    If you have questions or need assistance using the system, please visit our Contact Support page.
